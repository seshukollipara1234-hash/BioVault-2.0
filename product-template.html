SCIENTIFIC OVERVIEW

MECHANISM OF ACTION

HGH 191AA is a synthetic version of human growth hormone consisting of 191 amino acids, identical to naturally produced somatotropin. It stimulates the liver to produce IGF-1 (Insulin-like Growth Factor 1), which mediates many of its anabolic effects throughout the body.

EFFECTS ON HUMAN PHYSIOLOGY

  • Increases protein synthesis and nitrogen retention in muscle tissue
  • Promotes lipolysis and reduces adipose tissue accumulation
  • Enhances bone density through osteoblast stimulation
  • Improves collagen synthesis for skin and connective tissue
  • Accelerates wound healing and tissue regeneration
  • Enhances immune system function

CLINICAL APPLICATIONS

Originally developed for growth hormone deficiency treatment. Research applications include anti-aging protocols, muscle wasting conditions, bone density improvement, and metabolic optimization. Standard research dosing ranges from 2-4 IU daily for general applications to 6-8 IU for advanced protocols.

PHARMACOKINETICS

Half-life: 2-3 hours (subcutaneous injection)
Peak plasma levels: 3-5 hours post-administration
Bioavailability: 70-80% via subcutaneous route
Metabolism: Hepatic and renal clearance

STORAGE & HANDLING

Store lyophilized powder at 2-8°C. Once reconstituted with bacteriostatic water, maintain at 2-8°C and use within 14 days. Protect from light and avoid freezing. Handle with appropriate laboratory safety equipment.

SCIENTIFIC OVERVIEW

MECHANISM OF ACTION

Ipamorelin is a selective pentapeptide that stimulates endogenous growth hormone release via ghrelin receptor (GHS-R1a) activation. Unlike other GH secretagogues, Ipamorelin does not significantly affect prolactin or cortisol secretion. This action induces pulsatile GH release without overstimulation of other endocrine pathways.

NEUROLOGICAL AND COGNITIVE PATHWAYS

  • Interacts with hypothalamic ghrelin receptors to modulate growth hormone release
  • Potential influence on sleep architecture and REM cycles
  • May affect cognitive processing via GH-dependent IGF-1 pathways

RESEARCH APPLICATIONS

Ipamorelin is widely studied for applications in growth hormone regulation, anti-aging research, body composition studies, and neuroendocrine signaling. For laboratory research only. Not for human consumption.

PHARMACOKINETICS

Half-life: ~2 hours (in vivo)
Peak plasma GH: 30–60 minutes post-administration
Clearance: Primarily hepatic metabolism
Bioavailability: Dependent on administration route

STORAGE & HANDLING

Store lyophilized peptide at 2–8°C. Reconstitute with sterile water, maintain refrigeration, and use within 14 days. Avoid repeated freeze-thaw cycles.
